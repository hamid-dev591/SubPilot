Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if I forget my Master Password?

Because SubPilot is 100% local and encrypted, we cannot recover your password. If you lose it, your data remains encrypted and unreadable forever.

How can I backup my data?

Go to Dashboard > Settings. You can export your entire database as an unencrypted JSON file for safe keeping or transfer.
